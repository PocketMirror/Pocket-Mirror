About Us

Pocket Mirror is a four member emo, post-hardcore, metalcore, powerpop and indie rock band formed in Santa Cruz do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul, Brasil in 2021. The members are Matt Raven (vocals and guitar), Damon Spears (bass and backing vocals), Bruna Boo (drums) and Dini (guitar).

The target audience are specially teenagers, and one of the main goals is to use the current pop punk scene to create something a little bit more technical in original songs.
